Intro

My name is Firoz (fee-rose) and I'm a Frontend developer located in Anaheim, CA. I enjoy learning new things by speaking with people and by breaking and rebuilding things.

About Me

I wanted to build applications that weren't being built, solve problems that weren't being solved and be challenged (and frustrated) every day. So, I left my life in investment finance and found a new home in web development. I haven't been doing this very long, but can already say that it's the best decision I've ever made. I LOVE making things efficient- from simple things like teaching people keyboard short cuts to automating processes. Interests:Octopuses(trust me, it's not octopi), scuba diving, snow-kiting, sailing...really anything with the ocean. If I'm not in front of the computer, I'm usually on the water.
